About Me

I’m a visual effects artist as well as a 3D modeler and sculptor. I work in game dev in Unreal and Unity, using a wide range of software ( Houdini, Blender, Zbrush, Substance Package, and many others ). I’m an artist who loves new technologies, especially those connected to AR and VR. I’m trying to grasp as much as I can from the graphic, not only from 3d, but also from 2D and traditional graphics. Additionally, I’m a university teacher coz I love to share my knowledge and passion.
